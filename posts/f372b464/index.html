下载安装

赋予权限 chmod 755 chfs

CuteHttpFileServer文件共享下载-CuteHttpFileServer下载v2.0 官方免费版-软件下载软件下载,-六神源码网 (sctycp.cn)

//共享目录为/usr/local，监听端口号为8080
chfs –path=”/usr/local” –port=8080

//共享目录为”/home/jack/myshared”，监听端口号为80
chfs –path=”/home/jack/myshared”

//共享目录为”/home/jack/myshared”和”/home/java”，监听端口号为80
chfs –path=”/home/jack/myshared|/home/java”
